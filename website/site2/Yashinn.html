Yashin!

Lev Ivanovich Yashin - Soviet football player, goalkeeper. Olympic champion in 1956 and European champion in 1960, five-time champion of the USSR, three-time winner of the USSR Cup. Honored Master of Sports of the USSR (1957), Hero of Socialist Labor (1990).

Yashin managed to expand the understanding of the game in the goalkeeper's position that was customary for his time - he was one of the first in world football to regularly practice successful play on exits and use the entire penalty area. He was also distinguished by the ability to start attacks of his team, to lead the players in defense, which also became an innovation in those days. Recognized as the best goalkeeper of the 20th century by FIFA, IFFIIS, many newspapers and magazines. In 2003, the Russian Football Union named Yashin the best player in the country over the past 50 years.
