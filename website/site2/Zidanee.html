Zidane!

Zinedine Yazid Zidane - is a French footballer and coach. Considered one of the greatest players in football history. Zidane began his career at Cannes as a central midfielder, later played for Bordeaux, but the clubs that made the Frenchman famous throughout the world are Juventus and Real Madrid, in which the Frenchman won twelve trophies.

Zidane was named the best player in the world three times (1998, 2000, 2003) and once the best player in Europe (1998). In 2004, he was recognized by UEFA as the best football player of the last 50 years. In 2016, he was recognized as the best French footballer in history by France Football. His transfer to Real Madrid in 2001 for 75 million euros was the record amount paid for one player at that time, only 8 years later, in 2009, Zidane's record was broken by Cristiano Ronaldo, whose transfer was paid 94 million euros.
