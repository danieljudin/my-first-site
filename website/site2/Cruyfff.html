Cruyff!

Hendrik Johannes Cruyff - is a Dutch footballer and coach. As a player, he mainly played in attacking positions, becoming the first three-time winner of the Golden Ball and other awards. Considered one of the best players and coaches in the history of football.

Johan Cruyff is one of the most influential figures in the history of world football. The Dutch specialist's philosophy and style of play, both as a player and as a manager, influenced many future players and coaches. The successful performances of Spanish clubs and the Spanish national team in the period from 2008 to 2012 are also considered among the direct merits of Cruyff, since the influence of the philosophy of the Dutch specialist was reflected in the style of their game.
