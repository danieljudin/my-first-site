R9!

Ronaldo Luis Nazario de Lima or R9 is a Brazilian footballer who played as a striker. According to many football organizations, specialists and athletes, he is one of the best players in the history of football.

Two-time world champion, world vice-champion, the best football player in the history of the world championships according to the results of Castrol Index research, the best field football player of the 1998 and 2002 world championships, the best scorer of the 2002 world championship, the ex-record holder of the championships world in goals scored (surpassed in 2014 by Miroslav Klose), world championship record holder in the number of matches in which a football player scored at least one goal, record holder in the number goals scored in all international competitions, three times the best player of the year according to FIFA and the magazine World Soccer, two-time winner of the Golden Ball, winner of the Golden Boot, included in the list of the hundred greatest football players in the world in 13th place according to the magazine «World Soccer» (1999), in the FIFA 100 list, recognized as the best player of the decade in Serie A according to the Italian Football Association (1998-2007), the best player of the decade according to the results of voting on the goal.com portal (2000-2009), one of two players, along with Pele, who won the World Cup at 17, and one of four players, along with Romario, Geoffrey Hurst and Kylian Mbappe, who scored a hat-trick in the final of the international tournament of national teams.
