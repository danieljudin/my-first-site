Maradona!

Diego Armando Maradona - November 25, 2020, Dique Luján is an Argentine footballer who played as an attacking midfielder and striker. He played for the Argentinos Juniors, Boca Juniors, Barcelona, ​​Napoli, Sevilla and Newells Old Boys clubs, as well as the Argentina national team. World Champion 1986, Vice World Champion 1990. World Youth Champion 1979. The best player in the 1986 World Cup. South American Footballer of the Year 1979 and 1980. Twice member of the symbolic teams of the world championships.

Maradona is an honorary citizen of Buenos Aires and Naples. The stadium of the Argentinos Juniors club, opened on December 26, 2003 on the day of his debut in the team, and the stadium of the Napoli club are named after Diego. In June 2005, Maradona received the Faustino Sarmiento Award from the Argentine Senate.
