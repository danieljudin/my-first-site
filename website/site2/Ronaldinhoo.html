Ronaldinho!

Ronaldo de Asis Moreira - better known as Ronaldinho and Ronaldinho Gaucho, is a Brazilian footballer who played as an attacking midfielder and striker. World Champion (2002). Winner of the Golden Ball (2005). The best player in the world according to FIFA in 2004 and 2005.

Ronaldinho was born on March 21, 1980 in the San Lucas hospital in Porto Alegre, the capital of the state of Rio Grande do Sul, being the third child in the family. His mother, Dona Miguelina, worked as a saleswoman and trained as a nurse. His father, Jo√£o da Silva Moreira, was a shipyard worker and caretaker of the Gremio Club. Previously, he played for the local amateur team Cruzeiro. It was his father who became the first coach of Ronaldinho, teaching him the game of football, which he practiced on the streets of Vila Nova, where the family lived. In 1987, Ronaldinho began to study by going to Alberto Torres de Vila Nova College, but the love of football took its toll: Ronaldinho could play it all day, and even when his friends could not continue the game due to fatigue, he drove the ball along with with his dog Bombon.
